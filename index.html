SuDhama (सुधाम)

In Sanskrit, a "Good Home" or a "Good Place." For us, it is a sanctuary in the heart of Udupi, dedicated to reclaiming the slow life, rooted in tradition, nature, and affection for the land.

A Home Built from the Land

Architecture & Design

Built with locally sourced Laterite stone, the farmhouse embodies zero-mile construction. Its design respects the local climate with natural cross-ventilation and traditional clay tiles, creating a space that breathes with the land.

Sustainable Practices

Soil Health

We enrich our soil using only manure from our own cows and compost prepared on the farm.

Water Management

Drip irrigation conserves water, and rainwater harvesting pits replenish the groundwater.

Zero Chemicals

We are organic-certification ready, nurturing a healthy ecosystem free of pesticides.

The Second Innings: Our Journey

A Letter From Us

This land brought us back to ourselves. After decades in the city, we yearned for a life connected to the soil and seasons. SuDhama is more than a farm; it's the culmination of a dream—a place to nurture, heal, and live in harmony with nature. We are grateful to share its peace with you.
- Sudha & Girish

Founder's Note

"The decision to leave our urban life behind was not an easy one, but it was a necessary one. We felt a calling to return to our roots, to cultivate a life of purpose and intention. SuDhama is our answer to that call. It is a testament to the power of nature to heal, inspire, and transform. We hope that our story will inspire others to follow their own dreams, no matter how daunting they may seem."

- Sudha & Girish

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit the farm?

Yes, we welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us in advance to schedule your visit.

Is the milk from your cows for sale?

Currently, our milk is not for sale, but we are working on a small-batch, ethical dairy business. Stay tuned for updates!

Are you certified organic?

We are "organic-certification ready," which means we follow all organic practices, but we have not yet completed the official certification process.
